About Us

Who We Are?

We Help Clients To Get Success

We are a Financial Firm that offers Credit Counseling Services. We use a host of methods, such as authorized user tradelines, and various dispute methods. We have long-established relationships with all 3 major credit bureaus. Our team has over a total experience of 18 years in the industry. Spark Credit has helped people from all walks of life ages and backgrounds achieve their financial goals and improve their credit ratings.

For years we have removed Child Support, Student Loans, Repossessions, Collections, Foreclosures, Liens, Hard Inquiries, Charge offs, Bankruptcies, Judgements, late payments, and slow payments. Spark credit is an industry leader and the silver bullet against derogatory information in your credit profile. Don't let bad credit stop you from achieving your financial goals in life.
